Concept validation and start-ups

Client Issues

- Too many stakeholders involved in multiple projects.

- Complex scope and procurement process.

- Project benefits and objectives are not linked to the overall strategy or strategic plan.

- Absence of appropriate internal skills to manage 3rd party consultants.

- Measure results with target objectives.

Approach

1. DEFINE

- Define the strategic goals of the project and KPI's.

- Define a list with initiatives for change.

- Define a criteria for selection of 3rd party consultants.

- Assess the 3rd party consultants proposals.

2. PROCURE

- Assist in selecting the third party consultants.

- Communicate and document the third party consultant.

- Prepare project charter for the project.

- Develop Communication plan for stakeholders.

3. MONITOR

- Supervise the day to day work of the 3rd party consultants.

- Monitor the progress deliverables and ensure their aignment with the project charter.

- Report shortfalls and propose solutions.

4. EVALUATE

- Evaluate deliverables against the project goals.

- Identify shortfalls and propose corrective actions.

- Sign off the final deliverables.
